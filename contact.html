Contact Me

I'm happy to answer any computer questions you might have. Simply fill out the form below to get in touch and I'll respond when I get the chance. If you're looking to commission a custom PC, put yes in the corresponding box and leave a message explaining what you'd like out of your PC, such as the games you want to play or the work you expect to do. If you know even more about what you want, please leave more info. If you aren't sure what you want, let me know and I'm happy to discuss options to find out what works for you. Complete pricing will heavily depend on your component choices and be discussed more in our replies.
